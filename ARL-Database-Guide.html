6. Population Order & Checklist

Follow this order to avoid missing references:

Phase 1: Foundation

  • Create admin user(s) with appropriate roles
  • Fill in Site Settings (name, phone, email, address, social links)
  • Upload all Media assets (images for airlines, staff, news, pages)
  • Upload all Documents (PDFs for regulations, fees, tenders)

Phase 2: Content Collections

  • Add Airlines (with logos, destinations)
  • Add FAQs (all categories)
  • Add Notices (set status to published)
  • Add News & Events articles
  • Add Airport Project updates
  • Add custom Pages if needed
  • Add Flight overrides (only if API data is incorrect)

Phase 3: Site Page Globals

  • Home Page (hero text, emergency alert)
  • Passenger Guide (sections for check-in, baggage, security)
  • Transport & Parking (parking info, bus/taxi details)
  • Accessibility Info (wheelchair, assistance services)
  • Airport Map (points of interest with coordinates)
  • Contact Info (help desk cards with tel:/mailto: links)
  • Emergency Services (verified phone numbers — critical)
  • Working Hours & Directions (schedule, location, getting here)
  • VIP Lounge (amenities, booking, hours)
  • Regulations (sections with attached PDFs)
  • Usage Fees (fee categories with official PDFs)
  • Management & Staff (team profiles, departments)
  • Useful Links (grouped external links)

Phase 4: Legal & Final

  • Legal Pages: Disclaimer, Terms, Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy
  • Review all content in EN, FR, and MFE
  • Verify all emergency contact numbers
  • Test all tel: and mailto: links
  • Check all uploaded media displays correctly
  • Set all content status to published
Tip: Access the CMS admin panel at /admin to populate all content. Globals are under the “Globals” section in the sidebar. Collections are listed individually.
